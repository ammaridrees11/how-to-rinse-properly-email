Teaching your child to spit and rinse properly is an important step in their dental routine!

It helps them avoid swallowing toothpaste and keeps their mouth clean.

Practice Tips for Parents

•

Start with plain water before adding toothpaste
•

Count to five together while swishing
•

Under age 3: rice-grain smear only, no rinsing yet
•

Praise every attempt, even the messy ones!
